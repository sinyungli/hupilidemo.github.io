Criticise The Visual

Here is a flawed data visualization found online.

Good Points

  1. Clear: Show the trend by positioning the coordinates of the languages and comparing them to the line (expected trend).
  2. Informative: The scale of the image is large, the image includes much programming languages to be compared.

Bad Points

  1. Hard to read: All words are in black color plus the line is also in black, thus the words are packed closely.
  2. Boring: More description can be included, the image can also be made responsive to attract readers' interest.
