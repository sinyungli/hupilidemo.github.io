Criticise The Visual

Here is a flawed data visualization found online.

Good Points

  1. Clear: Show the numbers in text clearly
  2. Provides information: The graph tries to compare different types of users by presenting the figures in varied sizes of rings.

Bad Points

  1. Not factual: The sizes of the ring grows linearly instead of following the real growth rate.
  2. Boring: More description can be included, the image can also be made responsive to attract readers' interest.
